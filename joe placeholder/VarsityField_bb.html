Varsity Field

Quick Glance

Varsity Field has served as the University at Albany's on-campus baseball stadium for more than 30 years. The facility has been used throughout the program's transition from the NCAA Division III level to its present Division I affiliation. On March 24, 2000, Varsity Field hosted the school's first-ever Division I baseball contest, when the Great Danes defeated Canisius, 9-1. The Baseball Great Danes compete in the America East conference. Varsity Field is available to rent to non-commercial entities for games and practices at an hourly rate

Stadium has undergone many turn of the century enhancements including...

  • left field fence pushed back to 346 feet
  • left center pushed back to 388 feet
  • new warning track and foul poles
  • center field inning by inning scoreboard
  • bullpens and hitting cages beyond left field
