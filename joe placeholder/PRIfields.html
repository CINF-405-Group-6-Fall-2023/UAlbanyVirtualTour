Practice, Recreation and Intramural Fields

Quick Glance

In addition to the primary stadiums, UAlbany is also host to a number of less formal sporting fields used for practice, intramurals and general student use. Some of these fields at UAlbany are available to rent to non-commercial entities for various games and practices at an hourly rate

Dedicated sporting areas through athletic facilities including...

  • outdoor and indoor basketball courts
  • tennis courts
  • recreational softball and turf fields
  • new inning by inning scoreboard
  • Quad fields
  • low ropes course
