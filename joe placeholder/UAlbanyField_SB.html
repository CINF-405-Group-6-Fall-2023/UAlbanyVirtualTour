UAlbany Field

Quick Glance

UAlbany Field, just to the west of the Physical Education Building and Track & Field Complex, serves as the home venue for the UAlbany softball program. The facility has been used throughout the program's transition from NCAA Division III to its present Division I affiliation. UAlbany Field is available to rent to non-commercial entities for softball games and practices at an hourly rate.

Stadium has undergone many turn of the century enhancements including...

  • new foul poles
  • newly constrcuted bullpen
  • new backstop fencing and padding
  • new inning by inning scoreboard
  • new windscreens and dugout heaters
