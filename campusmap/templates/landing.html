UAlbany Virtual Tour

Welcome to our website!

Our website is designed to help you navigate the UAlbany campus!

You might be a graduating high-schooler, a newly-accepted freshman or transfer student, a worried parent, or even just someone who's simply interested in this school, and we understand that the University and its large in-person locations might be hard to navigate.

That's why we made this virtual tour - to help you get around the campus and learn more about its important locations and history!

Directions

  1. Click one of the links below.
  2. Hover over the areas that you are interested in. Their names will show up in the box on the lower right of your screen.
  3. Click that area to be taken to a page describing that location.

And that's all there is to it! Happy exploration!
